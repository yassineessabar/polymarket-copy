FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PolyX? ▼
PolyX is a Telegram bot that automatically copies trades from top-performing wallets on Polymarket, the largest prediction market. When a whale buys or sells a prediction, the bot mirrors the trade in your wallet with intelligent position sizing.
How does copy trading work? ▼
The bot polls target wallets every 5 seconds. When a new trade is detected, it calculates a confidence score based on the whale's bet size vs their history, then executes a proportionally-sized trade in your wallet. If the whale bets 1% of their portfolio, you bet 1% of yours.
Do I keep control of my funds? ▼
Yes. You hold your own Polygon wallet. The bot executes trades using your wallet's private key (encrypted and stored securely), but you can export your key, import your own wallet, or withdraw funds at any time. No custodial risk.
What are the fees? ▼
$39/month subscription for live trading, plus a 25% performance fee on profits only. If a position closes at a loss, you pay nothing extra. Demo mode is completely free with no limits. No transaction fees, no hidden costs.
How do I get started? ▼
Open the bot in Telegram, and you'll get a free wallet instantly. Start in demo mode to test with simulated funds. When ready, subscribe ($39/mo), deposit USDC on Polygon to your wallet, pick traders to copy, and the bot handles everything 24/7.
What risk protections are there? ▼
Six layers: daily loss halt (stops at -15%), drawdown scaling (reduces bets as losses accumulate), max position limits, per-event caps, total exposure limits, and correlation penalties. All configurable per user. The bot won't let you blow up.
